Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

We stand behind our work. This refund policy applies to services purchased from onXmariners.

1. Website & Design Services

For custom website projects, a 50% deposit is required to start. The deposit is non‑refundable after work has begun (wireframes, design concepts, or code written). If you cancel before any work has started, you will receive a full refund of the deposit.

2. Completion & Final Payment

Once the project is completed and delivered, the remaining balance is due within 7 days. No refunds will be issued after final delivery unless the website has critical bugs that we fail to fix within 14 days.

3. Content Creation & Consulting

For content packages or consulting calls, payment is due upfront. You may request a refund within 48 hours of purchase if no work has been performed. After that, no refunds are given.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email us at hello@onxmariners.com with your invoice number and reason. We will respond within 5 business days.

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without first requesting a refund, we reserve the right to suspend all services and pursue collection.
